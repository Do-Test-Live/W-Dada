About Lok On Leng Community Services

Lok On Leng Community Services is a non-profit charitable organization established under Section 88 of the Inland Revenue Ordinance (Registration Number: 91/16691). Operating under a social enterprise model, we are dedicated to elderly care and community services. We are also an approved service provider for the Social Welfare Department's "Elderly Community Care Service Voucher Pilot Scheme."


We are committed to creating conducive conditions for elderly individuals to age in place through various forms of nursing and care services. This includes providing both daytime and home care services. Our aim is to offer personalized services to seniors and caregivers based on their actual needs and capabilities. Additionally, we encourage their active participation in normal social and leisure activities within the community, enhancing their quality of life and achieving a holistic and healthy lifestyle.
